Our Core Features

Real Time

Measurements are constantly taken and processed in real time to ensure optimal conditions are maintained at all times.

Intelligent Control

Two sets of sensors are used to provide data on interior and exterior conditions. This allows us to intelligently combine control of your radiators, AC systems and windows for the most efficient control. Using an algorithm, weighted decisions are made based on user inputs and measured values.

5 Different Modes

Safe money and energy and support the environment. Our system manages room temperature and your windows optimally to reduce wasted energy.

User Interface

The intuitive mobile application allows desired levels to be adjusted to your liking and displays indoor conditions and control decisions.

Elegant Minimal Design

Our small sized system ensures that the interior design of your room will not be affected in any way.

Low cost

Our system has the lowest price and operational cost in the market.

Accurate Data

The usage of high precision sensors guarantees highly accurate measurements.

Always connected

Our low energy IoT system will measure and send data all the time and is always connected to our monitoring app.
